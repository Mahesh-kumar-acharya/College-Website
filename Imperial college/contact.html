Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

For BBA, you need 10+2 pass with minimum 50% marks. For BCA, you need 10+2 with Mathematics and minimum 50% marks. No entrance test or interview required.

When does the admission process start?

Admissions open in April every year. You can apply online through our website or visit our campus for direct admission.

What is the fee structure?

Please contact our admissions office for detailed fee structure. We also offer scholarship programs for deserving students.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with all modern amenities and 24/7 security.
